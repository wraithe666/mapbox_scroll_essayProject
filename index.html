Part 1

In this page we are going to see how interactions both positive and negative change as the partition occured as depicted in literary works. On would of course expect partition itself to be the time period with the a lot of negative interactions and pre-partition or long post partition to have more positive interactions.

Part 2

Pre-partition is mostly calm with few datapoints of interactions. We do see however that most of our stories are of course centered around the soon to be formed borders of East Pakistan, West Pakistan and India.

Part 3

During partition we see quite a few areas of negative interactions especially near the Punjab Border. A large part of the narrative of partition stories focusses on the experiences of characters during the partition. Though fictional these stories are potrayals of the very real horrors of partition faced by hundreds of thousands of people migrating into their 'new country'
